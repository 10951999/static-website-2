CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Adu-Atakora Justice is one of Ghana’s most rising photographers.
He is the CEO Founder of Alliswell Pictures and a Graphics Designer.
Whilst at young age, Justice realized his gift and passion for photography. He decided to turn his passion into
purpose and created his company Alliswell Pictures which is famously known for beautifully captured weddings,
lifestyle and excellent storytelling of events.
Justice believes in the power of storytelling and prides himself in creating visuals that leave his viewers with long-lasting memories.
He believes nothing is impossible as he says “with hard work, focus, consistency and God you too can make your dreams and goals a reality”
